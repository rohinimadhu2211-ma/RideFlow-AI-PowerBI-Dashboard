# RideFlow AI: Smart Mobility Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

RideFlow AI is an end-to-end Power BI analytics solution designed to analyze ride-hailing operations, revenue performance, and customer experience.

The dashboard provides actionable business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI monitoring.

## Objectives

- Monitor ride demand patterns
- Analyze revenue performance
- Evaluate operational efficiency
- Understand customer satisfaction
- Identify factors affecting cancellations

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Microsoft Excel
- CSV Dataset

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Dashboard

- Total Revenue
- Average Fare
- Cancellation Rate
- Driver Ratings
- Revenue by Pickup Zone

### 2. Operations Dashboard

- Ride Demand by Hour
- ETA Delay Analysis
- Traffic Impact Assessment
- Peak vs Non-Peak Analysis

### 3. Revenue Dashboard

- Revenue Trend by Hour
- Revenue by Pickup Zone
- Average Fare by Weather
- Surge Pricing Impact

### 4. Customer Experience Dashboard

- Customer Ratings Analysis
- Driver Ratings Analysis
- Ride Cancellations by Weather
- Customer Satisfaction by Pickup Zone

### 5. Key Business Insights

- Revenue Insights
- Operations Insights
- Customer Insights
- Strategic Recommendations

## Key Findings

- Anna Nagar generated the highest revenue.
- Revenue peaked during evening hours.
- High traffic conditions increased ETA delays.
- Peak hours contributed significantly to total rides.
- Average customer ratings remained above 3.9.
- Driver ratings remained consistently above 4.0.
